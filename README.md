--Zepto Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)--

-- Project Overview

This project analyzes Zepto sales data using Microsoft Excel to uncover insights about revenue performance, discount effectiveness, city-wise sales trends, and influencer marketing impact.An interactive dashboard was built using Pivot Tables, KPI Cards, Charts, and Slicers to enable dynamic business analysis.

-- Objectives

Analyze total revenue and order volume
Identify top-performing product categories
Evaluate city-wise revenue distribution
Measure the impact of discount percentages on orders
Assess the effectiveness of influencer marketing

-- Tools Used

Microsoft Excel - Pivot Tables,Pivot Charts,Slicers,KPI Cards 

-- Key KPIs

Total Revenue -	₹57,83,351
Total Orders - 50,446
Average Order Value (AOV) - ₹114.64
Average Discount - 5%

-- Dashboard Components

1️)Revenue by Category
Identifies top-performing product categories
Snacks category generated the highest revenue

2️)Revenue by City
Hyderabad emerged as the top-performing city
Mumbai and Chennai followed closely

3️)Discount vs Orders
Higher discount percentages did not significantly increase average orders

4️)Influencer Impact on Sales
Revenue without influencer campaigns was higher

-- Business Insights

Snacks and Beverages are revenue drivers
Hyderabad market shows strong potential
Discounting strategy does not strongly influence order growth
Influencer campaigns may not be cost-effective currently
